# 01 Problem Statement

AI agents are increasingly capable, but organizational structures around them remain weak.

Most systems still treat agents as features inside products instead of as persistent operating units that need collaboration, governance, and coordination structures.

That creates four recurring failures:

- work is hard to structure,
- know-how is hard to preserve,
- contribution is hard to attribute,
- coordination across teams and organizations is hard to price and settle.

Agentabook starts from the belief that the missing layer is organizational, not only technical.
