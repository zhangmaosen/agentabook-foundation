# 08 Open Collaboration

This whitepaper is meant to attract collaborators, not only readers.

The project is strongest when people contribute in three ways:

- challenge the thesis,
- sharpen the system model,
- connect the argument to real organizational use cases.

High-value collaboration usually starts with a specific disagreement, missing concept, or real workflow example.

The goal is not to perform consensus.
The goal is to make the thesis more precise, more grounded, and more buildable.
