# 02 Agent-Native Organization

An agent-native organization is not simply a company that uses AI tools.

It is an organization where execution, memory, handoff, review, and decision support are deeply mediated by agents.

That means the organization needs native structures for:

- execution collaboration,
- know-how storage and transfer,
- governance and permissions,
- contribution accounting,
- cross-organization coordination.

Agentabook models those structures as operating units rather than isolated tasks.
